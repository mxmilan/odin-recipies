Chicken Marsala

Description

Chicken Marsala is a creamy and rich italian sauce that has been present during my childhood. I enjoy this meal throughly when the sauce is creamy, and you dump it all over your chicken with a healthy amount of mushrooms littering the plate. It is an easy and fast meal to make when you're busy or just getting off of work. Bring some italian flavor to your kitchen with this wonderful recipie

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 4 med Chicken Breats
  • 4 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Tspn odried Oregano and Salt
  • 1 Cup sliced Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Cup Marsala Wine
  • 1/4 Cup Cooking Sherry

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, salt, pepper, oregano into dish
  2. Coat chicken in mixture
  3. Heat pan med heat, add olive oil, and lightly brown chicken
  4. Turn over chicken add mushrooms, wine, and sherry
  5. Simmer 10 min
