Chili

Description

This is my mother's famous chili bowl. I have had this chili ever since I was a kid. It is one of my favorite recipies. Usually I add avacado and crackers to make it even better. It is very easy to make and takes under 30 minutes. It has 4 different types of beans along with red onions and ground turkey. It is very useful for meal prepping, and gatherings as it serves a lot of food for a little amount of time.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Pound Ground Turkey
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Can Black Beans
  • 1 Can Chili Beans
  • 1 Can Rotele Tomatoes
  • 1 Can Condensed Tomatoe Soup

Steps:

  1. Dice Onion
  2. Put Turkey and 1/2 Onion in pot on med heat
  3. Add Chili Flakes and Red Pepper Spice
  4. Cook till meat isnt red
  5. Add rest of onion and cans
  6. Put heat on low
  7. Stur till done 20-ish minutes
