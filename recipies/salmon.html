Dijon Salmon

Description

Fantastic recipie that brings out plenty of flavor in the salmon. The mustard really makes the fish pop. My grandma made this recipie for a long time, my family adopted it. My brother was excellent at making it. This recipie is getting written down so i can also become a Dijon expert.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lemon
  • 3 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 4 Salmon Fillets
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Dry Breadcrumbs
  • 4 tspn parsley

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven to 400F
  2. together ingredients
  3. ingredients to top of salmon
  4. Bake 12-15 min or until flakey and add lemon
